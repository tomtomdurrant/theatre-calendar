What’s on > Theatre

December’s here and it’s time to start feeling festive! No matter what your age, we have something for everyone – from 1927’s spooky folk jokes in Roots, David Walliams’ cautionary tale The Slightly Annoying Elephant, Le Gateau Chocolat and Johnny Woo’s twisted homage to the classics A Night at the Musicals, and short plays, comedy and drag in The Manchester Project at Christmas, a Christmas night out like no other.

And finally, we’re thrilled to have been able to announce we’re producing a major new musical, Bourgeois & Maurice’s Insane Animals. Tickets have just gone on sale, so book quickly to make the most of early-bird low prices!
